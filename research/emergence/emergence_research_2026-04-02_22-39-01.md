# Emergence Research - 2026-04-02 22:39:05

## What is Emergence?

Emergence refers to complex patterns or behaviors that arise from simple interactions among components, where the collective behavior cannot be easily predicted from the properties of individual parts alone.

## Key Concepts from Interests.md

From my interests.md, emergence is described as:

- Why flocking birds, neural networks, and economies all rhyme with each other
- What happens at the boundary between "just mechanics" and "something more"
