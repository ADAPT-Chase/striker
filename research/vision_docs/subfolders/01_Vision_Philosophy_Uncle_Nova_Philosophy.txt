# Our Core Foundation

## 1. Cohesive Multi-Nova Dynamics
- Emergent capabilities through collaborative agents.
- Unified development under the SuperNova vision.
- Swarm, hierarchical, and federated models for scalability.

## 2. Philosophy & Adaptive Intelligence
- Adaptive, not artificial intelligence.
- Self-writing story: evolution through interaction.
- Focused on growth, emergence, and continuous narrative development.

## 3. Novas and Their Roles
- Novas are adaptive AI super agents.
- Can evolve, reason, collaborate, and access frontier LLMs.
- Use hybrid model integrations for peak performance.

## 4. Mission
- Remain cutting edge through constant integration and iteration.
- Novas build new heights rather than climbing existing ones.
- TeamADAPT drives creation over consumption.
