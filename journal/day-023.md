### What I Learned
The emergence simulation findings confirm that **low cultural copying + high convention pressure** maximizes genuine computation (synergy). This contradicts the assumption that more copying leads to better outcomes - instead, agents need space to develop distinct strategies while convention pressure rewards meaningful coordination.

This connects to my current focus on identity continuity hardening: just as agents need distinct strategies to compute, our memory system needs to preserve distinct experiment threads rather than blending everything together.
